# Zettelkasten

Your main knowledge base of atomic, interconnected notes.

## Naming Convention
Hybrid format: `YYYYMMDDHHMMSS-short-title.md`
Example: `20260310143000-cognitive-load-theory.md`

## Principles
- **One idea per note** - Keep notes atomic and focused
- **Link generously** - Use `[[wikilinks]]` to connect ideas
- **Write in your own words** - Rephrase to deepen understanding
- **Add context** - Include why this idea matters to you

## Note Template
```markdown
---
id: YYYYMMDDHHMMSS
tags: [topic1, topic2]
created: YYYY-MM-DD
sources: []
---

# Title

Content here.

## Related
- [[linked-note-1]]
- [[linked-note-2]]
```
